FAQ

Quick answers before you start

No pressure. No drama. Just a result you can use.

Is this a diagnosis

No. A test can not diagnose. It helps you understand intensity and pattern so you can choose a next step that fits.

What if my anxiety feels mostly physical

That is common. Anxiety often shows up through breath, chest, stomach, throat, dizziness, and tension. The questions include those signals.

Will this tell me if I have panic attacks

It can highlight a panic style pattern, meaning sudden surges that feel dangerous. The value is naming the pattern so you can respond differently and reduce fear of the next surge.

How long does it take

Most people finish in a few minutes. It is designed to be fast, clean, and readable.

Is my information private

This page is designed to feel respectful. Your answers are intended to be used for your result and your next step.

What should I do after I get my result

Follow the next step suggested for your level. If your result is higher and your life has been shrinking, structured support can speed up progress.
