Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026

Overview

MyAnxietyTest.com does not collect, store, or sell personal data. There are no user accounts, no registration forms, and no databases that hold information about you. When you use this site, you remain anonymous.

Analytics

This site uses Google Analytics to understand how people find and use the site. Google Analytics collects anonymised data including:

  • Pages visited and time spent on each page
  • General geographic location (country or city level)
  • Device type and browser
  • How you arrived at the site (search engine, direct link, referral)

This data is aggregated and anonymous. It is used solely to improve the site. Google Analytics is governed by Google's own privacy policy.

Affiliate links

Some links on this site are affiliate links, meaning we may earn a commission if you sign up for a service through them. Clicking an affiliate link may result in a cookie being stored by the third party (such as Online Therapy). We do not control or have access to those cookies. Please refer to the privacy policy of any third party service you choose to use.

Quiz and tool data

The quizzes and interactive tools on this site run entirely in your browser. Your answers are never sent to any server and are not stored anywhere. Closing the page clears everything.

Changes to this policy

This policy may be updated at any time without prior notice. Because we do not collect contact information, we have no way to notify users directly. We recommend checking this page periodically if you want to stay informed of any changes.

Contact

If you have any questions about privacy on this site, you can reach us through the r/anxiety_support community on Reddit.

Note: The tools and content on this site are for informational purposes only and do not constitute a clinical diagnosis. Some links on this site are affiliate links.
